Evergreen Hood River: Cannabis Supply
Medical & Recreational Marijuana Dispensary

We are an independent marijuana dispensary, serving medical and recreational customers 21 and over in Hood River, OR. Our inventory features local, organic, and sustainable products whenever possible: rare and artisan flower varieties, healthy, fresh edibles, potent concentrates, topicals, tinctures, CBD rich choices, and a small selection of accessories. Products are all Oregon made, and all flower is sourced from growers who do not use chemical pesticides or fertilizers. Everything is laboratory tested to ensure potency and safety.
