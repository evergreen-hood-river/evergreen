About Us

Hello!

We are an OLCC licensed marijuana dispensary, serving recreational and medical customers 21 and over in Hood River, OR. We are conveniently located in the Heights Business District, in a beautifully remodeled turn-of-the-century farmhouse. Our inventory features a well curated selection of flower, edibles, concentrates, and more. All items are laboratory tested to ensure purity and potency. Whether you enjoy cannabis recreationally or are seeking it as medicine, whether you are new to cannabis or are an experienced connoisseur, our knowledgeable staff is always happy to answer questions and to help you find the product that suits your needs.

We’re proud to be located in Hood River and the beautiful Columbia River Gorge. We hope to create a business that supports local farmers, environmental stewardship, and the end of prohibition.

Thank you! We look forward to meeting you.
